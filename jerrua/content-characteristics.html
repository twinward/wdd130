Jerrua: RPG Setting Guide

Characterisics

Demographics

Jerrua’s current urban population is about 120,000, with several surrounding rural towns and villages under its influence. The current population is nearly the smallest it’s been in several hundred years. Some 200 years ago, the population of Jerrua reached over 350,000, however after several bloody wars, a plague, and a mass exodus, the population dwindled to only about 90,000. Over the past 80 years, the city has regained some stability and begun recovering its population.

Geography

Jerrua sits along the western coast of Mara's Bay, about halfway down, just where it starts to narrow. Jerrua rules a region of about 3,200 square miles, stretching about 80 miles vertically along the coast, and spreading about 40 miles out from it.
