Jerrua

A Tabletop RPG Setting Guide

Overview

This site provides the citystate of Jerrua to tabletop RPG gamers and GMs as a prebuilt setting guide. It details the characteristics, economy, commerce, culture, religion, government, and history, as well as providing several NPCs and quest options. Jerrua is developed with enough detail to begin a game or campaign, while maintaining enough flexibility to steer the setting in their own direction.

Jerrua is among the largest and oldest cities in the world, founded immediately after the Mending of the World, when the Maidens (see Religion) brought mankind back to earth and governed them. For many centuries, Jerrua was the capital city of the kingdom of Jeraheim, however, nearly 200 years ago, Jeraheim entered a long period of civil wars, conquests, plague, and economic disaster. In that time, Jerrua lost all its territories to neighboring kingdoms and secession.
